## 💡 Key Takeaways

* **Dictionaries** store data as **key-value pairs**, making information easy to organize and retrieve.
* They provide **fast lookups**, making them one of Python's most powerful and frequently used data structures.
* Dictionary comprehensions and nested dictionaries enable clean solutions for handling complex and structured data.
